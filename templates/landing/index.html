Why choose the leading online ad network

Unique Partner Care

Our Partner Care Standards go beyond just answering questions. In addition to high-competent support, we offer guidance on optimizing campaigns and monetizing traffic. Expert managers assist in improving ad strategy for your benefit.

Ad Safety and Traffic Quality

Adsterra’s in-house 3-level security system prevents malware, ad fraud, and bot traffic, ensuring high-quality views and clicks. The platform’s intelligent algorithms serve offers to the most relevant audiences based on over 20 parameters.

Performance-Boosting Tools

Advertisers automate bidding strategies with Traffic Estimator and Smart CPM or set rules to optimize traffic by conversions (CPA Goal). Custom Bid allows for the finest bid adjustments, sending higher payouts to the best-performing placements.

Advertisers

Publishers
